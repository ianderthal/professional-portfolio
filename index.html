About Me

I am a front end developer living in mid-Michigan. I am most excited about mobile web usage and how our tablets & mobile devices are changing the way we use the web.

The presentation layer can impact the usage so much. Colors, imagery, iconography, and typography can enhance our users' experience. As a developer, I am entrusted to translate visuals into an interactive experience that displays across browsers and across displays.I've had some experience producing wireframes, working with clients to point them in the right direction.
